| Google Scholar | GitHub

I am an incoming Assistant Professor at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at Columbia University, working with Prof. Junfeng Yang at Columbia and Prof. Yinzhi Cao at Johns Hopkins University. I received my Ph.D. from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I was advised by Prof. Wei Meng.

My research focuses on software system security and its intersection with AI. My work addresses security challenges across web, cloud, and system software. Most recently, I build infrastructure to ground LLM-based software security agents with stronger guarantees.

I am looking for motivated students to work on broad security and privacy problems. Please see the openings for details.
